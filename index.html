To gain points help the dead have a nice Day of the Dead by choosing the right items for their altars

Beware! There are dogs around that like to steal things from the altars, don't click on them or you will lose points!

Also, make sure to not click on the dead and scare them away or you will lose a life

Get at least 20 points by the end of the game and you will win!

Save the Dead

"Save the Dead" is a game based on the Mexican tradition "The Day of the Dead". In this game, you have to click on the right items to decorate the altars without clicking on the dogs nor the dead because that will take away points and lives correspondingly.

This game was created by Ana Sofia Castellanos as part of the course 'Basic Animation' at Copenhagen School of Design and Technology.
